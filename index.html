SafeCuff: An Automated Cuff Pressure Monitoring and Control System for Enhanced Patient Safety

This project, SafeCuff, introduces an IoT based architecture for cuff pressure monitoring and control, as detailed in this study. SafeCuff effectively addresses the challenges associated with pressure fluctuations and enhances patient safety, especially in varying altitudes. I presented the work at an IEEE conference. The system's incorporation of remote notifications and dual sensor configurations ensures its reliability and user friendliness, delivering real time data to healthcare professionals for prompt intervention.

IoT Based Smart Irrigation System using DTMF and 8051 MCU

In this project, we proposed an IOT based Smart Irrigation System using DTMF (Dual Tone Multi Frequency) and 8051 MCU (MicrocontrollerUnit) as an innovative and transformative solution. This affordable system combines the power of DTMF technology with the versatility of the 8051 microcontrollers, enabling remote control and monitoring of the irrigation process. We published the paper in a national journal.
